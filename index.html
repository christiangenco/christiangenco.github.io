Hi! I'm Christian Genco, and this is my website. I make tiny software businesses, so I'm working on improving the way I make them. I'm focused on taking React courses so I can build stuff better and taking business courses to build better stuff. If you're interested, you can scroll down to Projects for a list of stuff I've made! When I'm not working, I'm pretty into minimalism (I keep a list of everything I own), and I hate driving, so I try to bike most places I need to go. Philosophically, I'm a mustachian, and hobby-wise I publish vlogs every once in a while as an excuse to fly my drone more, and I publish detailed notes on the most valuable books I read. If you're in the mood to learn everything about me you could listen to some of the Podcasts I've been on!

What I'm excited about right now

I tried doing standup comedy for the first time and it was a blast. I'm really excited about trying it more and improving my set! I'm also excited about launching the Microconf Recap Ebook, planning some outdoor rock climbing trips, and the new Fantastic Beasts movie.
