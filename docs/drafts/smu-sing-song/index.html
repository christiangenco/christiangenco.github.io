SMU Sing Song 2013

My second year of competing in Sing Song, I was cast as a surprise character an fulfilled a childhood dream of mine.

We actually ended up winning, becomming the first non-Greek organization to win in Sing Song's 25-year history.

It was a lot of hard work, but was very rewarding to see how much we improved each time we ran through it.

This scratched a long overdue itch for doing theatre in college.
